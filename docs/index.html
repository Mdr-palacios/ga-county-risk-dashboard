GA County Risk Dashboard Common Cause Georgia · Coalition data

Tracking immigration enforcement risk across all 159 Georgia counties.

A working dashboard for coalition partners. Combines public 287(g) agreements, license-plate surveillance networks (Flock), demographic data, HB 1105 status, and observed enforcement activity into a comparable risk score for every Georgia county. Use it to prioritize where to send legal observers, hotline capacity, and rapid-response volunteers.

Tiers: Critical ≥ 100; High 70 to 99; Medium 40 to 69; Low < 40. v3 model adds observed arrests and surveillance mesh on top of the v2 demographic base. The model is directional, not predictive: a county scoring "Medium" is not safe; it just has fewer of the indicators we can track from public sources.
